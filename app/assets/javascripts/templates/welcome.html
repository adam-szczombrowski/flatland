TIMELINE

How to play?

TIMELINE is a great educational game. In order to win you have to place 6 inventions in chronological order. You are going to ask yourself: was the light bulb invented before the bicycle? And what about the telegraph?

You get points for correctly guessing in which point in time invention on the card on the bottom of the screen took place. You win the game when you score 6 points!

Click the button to start the game.
